# 常用命令

1. 杀死进程
#####查找tomcat进程  
ps -ef | grep tomcat | grep -v grep | awk '{print $2}'  
#####查找tomcat进程并杀死  
ps -ef | grep tomcat | grep -v grep | awk '{print $2}' | xargs kill -9
